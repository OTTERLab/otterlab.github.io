Our Mission

At Open-source Technologies & Theory for Experimental Research (OTTER) Laboratories (Labs), we push the boundaries of autonomous systems through innovative research at the intersection of robotics, computer vision, and artificial intelligence. Our approach combines rigorous theoretical foundations with practical experimentation to develop next-generation autonomous technologies.
