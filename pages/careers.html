Careers

We don’t just hire employees; we enlist pioneers and problem-solvers ready to shape the future. If you think you’re one of them, here’s what to expect.

The path to joining OTTER Laboratories starts with a tailored interview, focused on the role you’re pursuing. We don’t do generic questions—your interview will dig deep into the skills, ingenuity, and drive you bring to the table. If you pass the first stage, you’ll join us for one to two weeks, working directly on real projects that mirror the intensity of our mission. This isn't a test; it's a preview of the commitment required to be part of our team.

If the team sees you’re a fit, you’ll get continuous feedback to refine and advance your impact. We believe in transparency and growth at every stage, so you’ll know exactly where you stand and how you’re contributing to our shared goals.

At OTTER Laboratories, we’re driven by the relentless pursuit of breakthroughs. We want people who thrive on experimentation, who push boundaries, and who dive into unknowns without hesitation. You'll be responsible not only for your work but also for meticulously documenting your progress through detailed reports, research journals, and logs. These records aren’t just for us—they’re to capture the legacy of what we build together.

This isn’t a typical job; it’s a calling. We’re looking for people ready to take ownership, break new ground, and turn ambitious ideas into reality. Ready to prove yourself? Apply, and let’s see if you have what it takes.
